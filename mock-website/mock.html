> About

For over 10 years, I've built systems where failure isn't an option: mission-critical legal-tech infrastructure serving 100,000+ lawyers filing directly to Italian courts. I find what actually needs solving, not just what I'm told to build. My real job isn't just writing code, but protecting your business from expensive technical mistakes.

Before we write a line of code, I'll give you a clear picture of what's realistic, where the risks are, and what it will actually cost you. No sugarcoating, no hidden surprises.

Falling behind doesn't happen all at once. It happens one small wrong decision at a time: a system that breaks under load, a rebuild you didn't budget for. And while you're putting out those fires, your competitors are already moving: automating, innovating, adopting AI.

If your tech is keeping you up at night, or if you know it soon will, I'm the call you make before it gets expensive.
